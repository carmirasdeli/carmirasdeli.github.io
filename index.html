Our Story

Our Family has been serving Delaware County, slicing the freshest meats and cheesees and finest Hoagies, for over 47 years!

Car-Mira's derived from our founding families 2 combined surnames; Carmilingo and Mirasola, Operating under the Mirasola family for 3 generations starting when Vincetta Mirasola opened its doors in 1975!
